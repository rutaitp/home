When home has been so taken or never given to so many of us for so long

We can be home for each other

I am trying to be home for everyone I meet

If even

For a moment

Lay your guard down

Our guns down

Let your shoulders rise

Bagless

The unbearable lightness of being

Home

I will be honest with you and offer that you

Can do the same with me

And I will

Love you to the best of my ability

Home

Let us provide

For each other

Home in the micro moments

In which our humanness is most distinct

We can make houses with the raw material the earth has birthed for us

To make our second home of and within our first[ What about the womb?]

But we can also make home amongst each other

I am trying to smile safety to you

You need not worry here

With me

I am nowhere near perfect

But I am
